Our History

Xabta, founded in 2016, is an IT company with experienced founders who aim to provide valuable services in the rapidly growing technology industry.

Xabta is a software development company specializing in custom solutions and website applications, ensuring client satisfaction and growth for small and medium-sized companies in the online world.

Xabta is dedicated to assisting small businesses in becoming successful online entities, serving as their IT department, generating business leads, and increasing global sales through top-notch services.
